About MultiLearn

Supporting Grundschule students with multilingual tutoring across Berlin

Our Mission

MultiLearn connects Grundschule students with qualified tutors who can teach in their preferred language. We understand the challenges of learning in a multilingual environment and aim to make education accessible to all students in Berlin.

Our Vision

We envision a learning environment where language is not a barrier to education. Our platform helps students succeed in their studies while maintaining a connection to their native language.

What Sets Us Apart

Multilingual Support

Our tutors can teach in various languages, helping students learn in the language they're most comfortable with.

Qualified Teachers

All our tutors are experienced educators with proven track records in helping Grundschule students.

Local Focus

Our platform connects you with tutors in your neighborhood, making in-person tutoring convenient.
